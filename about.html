About Leo

Hello, I'm Leo, a Senior Cloud Engineer and Architect with over a decade of experience in the tech industry. My journey began with a deep fascination for technology and a passion for innovation. Join me on this exciting journey as we push the boundaries of what's possible in the cloud.
